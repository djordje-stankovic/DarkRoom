FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is NAVI and how does it work? +

NAVI is a comprehensive online property navigator that combines interactive property exploration with cutting-edge visualization technology. It delivers immersive virtual tours, smart filtering, and detailed floor plans — all accessible from any device.

Is NAVI accessible on mobile devices? +

Yes! NAVI is fully responsive and works seamlessly across smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface adapts to each device for an optimal viewing experience.

Can NAVI be customized for our brand? +

Absolutely. NAVI can be fully branded to match your company's identity, including colors, logos, and custom UI elements. We work closely with each client to ensure a cohesive experience.

What types of properties does NAVI support? +

NAVI supports all property types — from residential apartments and houses to commercial spaces and large-scale developments. It's designed to scale with your project needs.

How is NAVI priced? +

Pricing varies based on project scope, number of properties, and customization needs. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your specific requirements.
