Cinemagraphs

Where renderings and animation clash — hybrid still images with subtle, mesmerizing motion.

A cinemagraph is a blend between a still photo and a video. It's primarily a photograph, but it has a minor and repeated movement occurring, which makes it look like a short video loop. This unique medium captures attention in ways that neither static images nor full videos can — creating an almost magical quality that draws viewers in and holds their gaze.

Perfect for social media, presentations, and marketing materials where you want to stand out.
