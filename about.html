Our Story

From Architects to Visualization Artists

Darkroom Studio began as a small team of architects exploring the intersection of design and technology. What started as an experiment quickly evolved into a passion — creating photorealistic visualizations that help architects, developers, and designers communicate their vision with clarity and impact.

Today, we specialize in high-quality still images, cinematic animations, and cutting-edge virtual reality experiences. Our team of 20+ artists pushes the boundaries of architectural visualization, creating impactful narratives that truly resonate with audiences worldwide.

Based in Niš, Serbia, we serve clients across America, Europe, and Oceania — delivering world-class quality with a personal touch.
